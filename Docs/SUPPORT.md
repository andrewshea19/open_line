# Open.Line Support

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I add friends?
Tap the Friends tab, then tap the "+" button. Enter your friend's phone number to send a friend request. They'll need to accept before you can see each other's status.

### Why can't I see my friend's status?
Make sure:
1. They have accepted your friend request
2. They have the app installed and are signed in
3. You both have internet connectivity

### How do I schedule my availability?
1. Go to the Status tab
2. Tap "Add Schedule"
3. Choose recurring (weekly) or one-time event
4. Set the days, times, and status

### How do I change my status quickly?
Tap the status indicator on the main screen to cycle through Available → Unavailable → No Status. You can also use the home screen widget.

### Why isn't the widget updating?
Widgets refresh every 5 minutes. For immediate updates, open the app briefly. Make sure the app has background refresh enabled in Settings.

### How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings → Delete Account. This removes all your data from iCloud.
