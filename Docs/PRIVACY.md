# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 2026**

## Overview

Open.Line ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Name**: Used to identify you to friends
- **Phone Number**: Used to connect with friends and enable calling
- **Email** (optional): Used for account identification

### Automatically Collected
- **Availability Status**: Your current status (Available, Unavailable, No Status)
- **Status Schedule**: Times you've scheduled to be available
- **Device Token**: For push notifications (if enabled)

## How We Use Your Data

- Display your availability to approved friends
- Send notifications when friends become available
- Sync your status across your devices

## Data Storage

All data is stored securely in Apple's iCloud (CloudKit). We do not operate our own servers. Your data is:
- Encrypted in transit and at rest
- Stored in your personal iCloud account
- Only shared with friends you explicitly approve

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share data with advertisers
- Track your location
- Access your contacts without permission

Your availability status is ONLY visible to friends you have mutually connected with in the app.

## Your Rights

You can:
- Delete your account and all data at any time
- Remove friends to stop sharing your status with them
- Disable notifications
- Export your data via iCloud

## Contact

For privacy questions or concerns:
- Open an issue: [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/yourusername/OpenLine/issues)
- Email: support@openlineapp.com

## Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated through the app.
